Request for NSF GRFP materials

I am happy to make my NSF GRFP essays available to PhD applicants in economics and related fields. To request the materials, please send an email to me with some basic information on who you are, confirm you are applying in the next cycle or two, and affirm that you will not share my essays with others. Please use the subject line: "Request for NSF GRFP materials".
